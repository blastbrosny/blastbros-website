Our Story

Three best friends. One hose. Big plans.

Blast Bros started in a driveway with a bucket, a hose, and an idea: why pay full price for a wash when three kids with a work ethic could do it better — and have more fun doing it?

Now we wash cars and blast driveways clean all over the neighborhood, one block at a time. Every dollar earned goes right back into gear, school, and savings — and every customer gets the same thing: kids who actually care about doing the job right.

🧒 Kid-Owned & Operated 🚐 We Come to You 💧 Eco-Friendly Soap

Our promise is simple: show up on time, work hard, and leave your car or driveway spotless. No shortcuts, no excuses — just three kids who take pride in a job well done.
